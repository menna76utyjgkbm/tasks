Chicken Shawarma

A popular Middle Eastern street food made with spiced chicken, wrapped in soft bread and served with garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic sauce
  • Pita bread
  • Spices (paprika, cumin, turmeric)
  • Salad (lettuce, tomato, onion)

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  2. Cook the chicken until golden brown.
  3. Warm the pita bread.
  4. Add chicken, salad, and sauce.
  5. Wrap and serve hot.
